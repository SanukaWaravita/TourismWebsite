Riverston

Riverston is a scenic mountain area in Sri Lanka known for its cool climate, lush landscapes, and hiking spots like Mini World's End and Thelgamu Oya. It's ideal for nature lovers and peaceful getaways.

Sigiriya

Sigiriya, also known as the “Lion Rock,” is a 5th-century rock fortress in central Sri Lanka, famed for its frescoes, lion’s paw entrance, and panoramic summit views. It’s a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a masterpiece of ancient architecture.

Temple of the Tooth Relic

The Temple of the Tooth Relic in Kandy is a revered Buddhist site that houses Lord Buddha’s tooth relic. Known for its stunning architecture and cultural significance, it also hosts the famous Esala Perahera festival.

Ambuluwawa

Ambuluwawa is a multi-religious tower and ecological site in Gampola, Sri Lanka, known for its spiral tower with panoramic views and symbols of religious harmony. It's a popular spot for nature lovers and adventurers.

1. What is the best time to visit Sri Lanka?
The best time depends on the region. For the west and south coasts, December to March is ideal. For the east coast, May to September is best due to dry weather.
2. Do I need a visa to travel to Sri Lanka?
Yes, most travelers need an Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) before arrival. You can apply online via the official Sri Lanka immigration website.
3. What are the top attractions in Sri Lanka?
Some must-see places include: Sigiriya Rock Fortress Temple of the Tooth Relic (Kandy) Ella & Nine Arches Bridge Yala National Park Galle Fort Mirissa and Unawatuna beaches Dambulla Cave Temple
